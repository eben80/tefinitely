Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2024

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how TEFinitely ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you register, we collect your name and email address.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use our site, including practice sessions and flashcard progress.
  • Technical Data: We collect IP addresses, browser types, and operating systems to ensure compatibility and security.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to maintain your session and remember your preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain our Service.
  • Personalize your training experience.
  • Process payments and subscriptions.
  • Communicate with you about your account and updates.
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends.

4. Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your password is encrypted. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with third-party service providers (like PayPal for payments or OpenAI for AI features) as necessary to provide our service. These providers are bound by their own privacy policies.

6. Cookies and Tracking

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the site.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can manage most of your data through your profile page or contact us for assistance.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at tefinitely@gmail.com.
